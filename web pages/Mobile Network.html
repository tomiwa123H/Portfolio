What We Do

Mobile Infrastructure Done Properly

From initial feasibility through to final commissioning, we manage every stage of your mobile network deployment — so nothing gets missed.

5G Cell Site Design

End-to-end planning for next-generation 5G sites — macro towers, small cells and DAS systems for high-density environments.

RF & Microwave Engineering

Expert planning for point-to-point and backhaul links using licensed and unlicensed spectrum — across any terrain or environment.

Site Acquisition & Build

Surveying, planning applications, landlord negotiation, physical build and commissioning — we manage the whole lifecycle.

Radio Network Planning

Optimised coverage and capacity design for 4G LTE and 5G NR deployments — rural, urban or enterprise environments.

Our Approach

From Planning to Live Network

Mobile network deployment involves a lot of moving parts — planning authorities, landlords, equipment vendors and network operators. Our team coordinates all of it, so you have a single point of accountability throughout.

With decades of experience across national operator programmes and private network builds, we know how to keep complex projects on track without cutting corners.

  • Macro, micro and small cell design
  • DAS for high-density environments
  • Private 4G/5G for enterprise campuses
  • Ofcom licensing and planning support
  • Drive testing and post-deployment optimisation
  • Ongoing O&M support available
